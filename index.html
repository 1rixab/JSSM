Why Recycle Your Vehicle

Recycling End-of-Life-Vehicles (ELV) helps reduce air and water pollution that is otherwise emitted in the production of new vehicles. It helps conserve fossil fuels required for steel production.

Recycling vehicles also saves energy that would be used for creating new materials. Recycling steel requires 74% less energy compared to making new steel.

Recycling your vehicle helps save natural resources. The use of scrap metal replaces a significant part of mining for iron ore, coal, and other materials.

Required documents and a legally valid certificate of destruction is issued to the owner to help with deregistration of vehicle from the relevant R.T.O.
